# Proposed Onboarding Flow

This document describes a proposed onboarding flow for new users of the Onyx app. The goal is to introduce users to the app's core concepts—voice-driven AI commands, community data marketplace, MCP & DVM integration, Bitcoin wallet setup, and navigating the AI orb interface—over a short series of 8-10 screens.

## Onboarding Goals

1. Familiarize users with the Onyx orb and voice-driven commands.
2. Explain the AI marketplace concept (MCP & DVM).
3. Demonstrate the community-driven intelligence aspect.
4. Guide users through setting up their Bitcoin wallet for payments.
5. Ensure users understand privacy, security, and consent mechanisms.
6. Provide a smooth transition into the main interface.

## Flow Overview

**Number of screens:** 8-10
**Approximate Time:** ~2-3 minutes total

## Screen-by-Screen Outline

### Screen 1: Welcome & Introduction

- **Focus:** High-level overview of Onyx.
- **Content:**
  - A simple welcome message: "Welcome to Onyx."
  - A brief description: "Onyx is your voice-driven AI agent that can find and process information from a decentralized network."
  - Reassure with a “no-coding” vibe, just speak commands.
- **CTA:** "Next" button.

### Screen 2: Voice-First Interaction

- **Focus:** Introduce the voice commands.
- **Content:**
  - Explain: "You speak, Onyx listens and responds."
  - Show a mock command: “Tell me about recent drone sightings in Colorado.”
  - Mention automatic transcription and chat integration.
- **CTA:** "Next" button.

### Screen 3: The Onyx Orb

- **Focus:** The visual representation of the AI.
- **Content:**
  - Display a stylized image of the Onyx orb.
  - "This orb represents Onyx. When it pulses, it's thinking!"
  - Mention that tapping the orb may reveal more details or trigger certain actions.
- **CTA:** "Next" button.

### Screen 4: Data Marketplace (MCP & DVM)

- **Focus:** Explain the concept of requesting data via marketplace.
- **Content:**
  - "Onyx uses a data marketplace to find and pay for specialized AI services or datasets."
  - Briefly mention MCP & DVM: "MCP connects us to tools & data sources; DVM is a marketplace of AI services."
  - Highlight that users can post requests and receive results from different providers.
- **CTA:** "Next" button.

### Screen 5: Community Intelligence

- **Focus:** Show community contributions.
- **Content:**
  - "Community members submit data about drone sightings. The more data submitted, the smarter Onyx becomes."
  - Mention verified reports and trust signals.
- **CTA:** "Next" button.

### Screen 6: Bitcoin Wallet Setup

- **Focus:** Guide them through wallet initialization.
- **Content:**
  - "Onyx integrates with Bitcoin & Lightning. Earn or pay sats for services."
  - Ask user to create or restore a mnemonic (seed phrase).
  - Mention breez SDK for seamless Lightning integration.
- **Actions:**
  - Button: "Set Up Wallet" → leads to mnemonic generation or restoration flow.
  - If restoring a wallet, a text input for mnemonic is shown.
- **CTA:** "Next" after completion.

### Screen 7: Privacy & Security

- **Focus:** Emphasize data protection and consent.
- **Content:**
  - "Your data is yours. Onyx only shares what you approve."
  - Mention NIP-42 (authentication), encryption for events, and no uploading voice data without consent.
- **CTA:** "Next" button.

### Screen 8: Adjusting Preferences

- **Focus:** Show how to tweak settings.
- **Content:**
  - "Customize your experience: choose data formats, filter community reports, manage tool permissions."
  - Show a small mock of a preferences panel (e.g., include anomalies, pick JSON output).
- **CTA:** "Next" button.

### Screen 9: Trying a Command

- **Focus:** Quick practice.
- **Content:**
  - "Try a voice command now!"
  - Prompt: “Ask: ‘What drone data is available for January 2024 in Denver?’”
  - User can skip if they want.
- **CTA:** "Try Now" or "Skip" → On success, show result briefly or move on.

### Screen 10: Ready to Explore

- **Focus:** Wrap-up and go to main UI.
- **Content:**
  - "You're ready! Open the tabs at the bottom to explore Marketplace, Analysis, Community, and Feedback."
  - Encouraging message: “Speak freely. Onyx will handle the rest!”
- **CTA:** "Get Started" → transitions to main app interface (tabs with Onyx orb visible).

## After Onboarding

- User lands in the main Onyx interface:
  - Tabs: Marketplace, Analysis, Community, Feedback.
  - Onyx orb on the home screen ready for voice commands.
  - Wallet accessible from profile or wallet screen.
  - Clear paths to request data, view community reports, and experiment with voice queries.

---

This onboarding flow ensures users understand the unique concepts Onyx introduces—voice AI commands, open agent marketplace (MCP/DVM), community intelligence, and Bitcoin wallet setup—before exploring the full functionalities of the app.
